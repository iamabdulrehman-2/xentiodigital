SECTION 9: FAQs

SECTION LABEL

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

SECTION HEADLINE

Everything You Want to Know Before Working With Us

Q1: How quickly can I start seeing results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term investment, not an overnight switch. Most businesses start seeing meaningful movement in rankings within 3 to 4 months, with significant results typically visible at the 6-month mark. The timeline depends on your industry competition, your website's current state, and the strategy we build together. We will give you a realistic projection.

Q2: Do you work with businesses outside Pakistan?

Yes, absolutely. Xentio Digital serves clients in the US, UK, UAE, Canada, Australia, and across Europe and Asia. Digital marketing works across borders and so do we. All communication is done in English, and we adjust our working hours to accommodate different time zones.

Q3: How much should I budget for paid ads?

Your ad spend is separate from our management fee. The right budget depends on your industry, competition, and goals, but for most businesses, we recommend starting with at least $300–$800/month for Meta Ads and around $500–$1,500/month for Google Ads to generate reliable data and meaningful results.
For local businesses in Pakistan, even smaller budgets can work well when campaigns are targeted correctly. During your consultation, we’ll suggest a realistic budget based on your market and growth goals — not just a random number.

Q4: What platforms do you build websites on?

We build websites on WordPress, Shopify, and fully custom platforms using modern frameworks. We recommend WordPress for service businesses, blogs, and informational sites. Shopify is our first recommendation for ecommerce businesses. For complex requirements, we build fully custom solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Q5: Do you require long-term contracts?

We do not lock you into long-term contracts. Our services run on a monthly basis and you can cancel with 30 days notice. We prefer to earn your business every month through results rather than legal obligations. That said, SEO in particular requires a minimum of 3 to 6 months to show meaningful results, so we encourage clients to commit to that initial window.

Q6: How do you measure and report results?

Every client gets a weekly performance report. For SEO clients, we track keyword rankings, organic traffic, backlinks earned, and conversion metrics. For paid ads clients, we report on impressions, clicks, click-through rate, cost per click, conversions, and return on ad spend. You always know exactly what your investment is returning.

Q7: What industries do you work with?

We work with businesses in every industry. Ecommerce, retail, health and wellness, finance, real estate, education, SaaS, legal services, hospitality, food and beverage, automotive, fashion, B2B, manufacturing, and many more. Our SEO and paid advertising strategies are adapted to your specific industry, your competition, and your customers.

Q8: Can you help a business that is starting from zero online presence?

Yes. A significant number of our clients come to us with little to no online presence. We build everything from the ground up: the website, the SEO foundation, the ad accounts, and the content strategy. Starting from scratch actually has an advantage: we get to build it right from the beginning, without fixing someone else's mistakes first.

Q9: How do I get started with Xentio Digital?

It starts with a free consultation. Fill in the contact form on this page, and tell us a bit about your business and your goals. We will contact you back within 24 hours, audit your current situation, and present a clear plan. No pressure, no hard sell. Just an honest conversation about how we can help.

Q10: What information do you need from me to get started?

To give you the most useful strategy call, it helps to know: what your business does and who your ideal customers are, what you have tried before in marketing and what the results were, your main goal right now (more traffic, more leads, more sales, or a new website), and your monthly budget range. You do not need to have all the answers. That is what the consultation is for.
